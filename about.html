Our Story

Rooted in Legacy, Built for the Future

Tolbert Innovation Hub is a youth-focused innovation and empowerment organization committed to transforming lives through education, skills development, technology, and opportunity access.

Inspired by the legacy of President William V. S. Tolbert — a Liberian leader who championed education, agricultural self-reliance, and Pan-African solidarity — the Hub was established to create a future where every Liberian, regardless of background, has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

We believe that the seeds of Liberia's transformation lie in the hands of its young people. By bridging access gaps in education, healthcare coordination, and technology, we are building a new generation of leaders equipped to drive lasting change across the continent.

From scholarship pathways to free classroom resources, international healthcare exposure, and software innovation support — every initiative we run is designed with one goal in mind: to make opportunity universal.

"Our mandate is clear: educate, empower, and elevate. We will not rest until every Liberian young person has a genuine pathway to their full potential."

— Tolbert Innovation Hub Leadership
