What to Look For

Finding the Certificate ID & QR Code

Every TIH Learning Hub certificate has two verification features: a unique alphanumeric Certificate ID in the lower section, and a QR code that links directly to this verification portal.

  • 1️⃣Certificate ID: Found in the bottom section, formatted as TIH-YYYY-XXXXXX
  • 2️⃣QR Code: Located at the bottom-right corner of the certificate
  • 3️⃣Verification Stamp: A gold "VERIFIED" badge with the institution seal
