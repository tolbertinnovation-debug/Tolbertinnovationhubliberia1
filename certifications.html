Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Are TIH Academy certificates recognized by employers?+
Yes. TIH Academy certificates are recognized by government agencies, NGOs, private-sector employers, and international organizations across Liberia and the broader West Africa region. We partner with 500+ employers to ensure our certifications meet real-world standards.
How long does it take to earn a certificate?+
Program duration varies from 3 to 12 months depending on the program track. Since you learn at your own pace, some learners complete programs faster. There are no strict deadlines — you have lifetime access to all materials.
What format is the certificate delivered in?+
You receive a high-quality digital certificate (PDF) immediately upon completion. Each certificate includes a unique verification QR code and certificate ID. Physical printed certificates are also available for an additional fee and can be shipped to your address.
Do certificates expire?+
TIH Academy certificates do not expire. Once earned, your certificate remains valid and verifiable for life. Your employer can verify it at any time using our online verification portal at no cost.
Can I share my certificate on LinkedIn?+
Absolutely. Each certificate includes a LinkedIn share button that automatically populates your credential details. You can also manually add it to your LinkedIn profile using the certificate name, organization, and issue date provided in your certificate PDF.
Is financial aid available for certificate programs?+
Yes. TIH Academy offers scholarships, payment plans, and financial aid for eligible learners who demonstrate financial need. Apply through our scholarship page or contact our support team. We believe cost should never be a barrier to education.
