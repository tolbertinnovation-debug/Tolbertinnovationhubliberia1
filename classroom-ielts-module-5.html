Module Objectives

  • Handle Part 1 personal questions naturally.

Module 5 Quick Start

  • Part 1: Give short natural answers and extend with one reason or example.
  • Part 2: Use 1-minute planning to note 4–5 keywords before speaking.
  • Part 3: Build answers with opinion + reason + example + mini conclusion.
  • Daily speaking: Record, review, and correct fluency and pronunciation errors.

Speaking Strategy Guide

Part 1: Introduction & Interview

Give brief, natural, and relevant answers. Extend with one reason or example. Do not over-prepare memorized scripts — natural fluency scores higher.

Part 2: Cue Card (Long Turn)

Use preparation time to jot 4–5 keywords. Structure: intro → main points → example → short conclusion. Speak for the full 1–2 minutes.

Part 3: Discussion

Give reasoned, developed answers. Pattern: Opinion + Reason + Example + Compare or Contrast. Avoid one-word replies — Part 3 rewards depth.
