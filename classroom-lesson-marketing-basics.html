What Is Marketing?

Marketing is everything you do to attract customers and convince them to buy from you. It is not just advertising. It includes: understanding what customers want, creating a product or service that meets that need, communicating its value clearly, delivering a good experience that makes customers return and refer others. Good marketing does not require a big budget - it requires understanding your customer deeply.

Social Media Marketing in Liberia

Facebook and WhatsApp are the dominant digital platforms in Liberia. Facebook: Create a business page with a professional profile photo, contact information, location, and business hours. Post consistently - at least 3 times per week. Share photos of your products, customer testimonials, tips, and behind-the-scenes content. WhatsApp Business: Set up a business profile and broadcast list. Share offers, new products, and updates directly with customers who have opted in. Use WhatsApp status for daily product or promotion visibility.

Word of Mouth and Community Marketing

In Liberia, referrals and personal recommendations are the most powerful form of marketing. Strategies: (1) Provide exceptional service - satisfied customers tell others. (2) Ask happy customers to refer friends and offer them a small incentive. (3) Partner with community leaders, churches, mosques, schools, or local organizations to reach their networks. (4) Sponsor or participate in local events to increase visibility. (5) Create simple flyers and distribute them in high-traffic areas.

The Basics of Pricing Your Product or Service

Price too high and you lose customers. Price too low and you lose money. A simple pricing approach: Calculate your total cost per unit (materials + labor + overhead). Add your desired profit margin (typically 20-50% for products, 30-80% for services). Research what competitors charge. Test your price with real customers and adjust based on feedback. Never compete on price alone - compete on quality, trust, speed, or convenience.

💡 Key Tips

  • Post real photos of your actual products and real customer testimonials - authenticity builds trust faster than polished advertising.
  • Respond to every message and comment on social media within a few hours - fast responses build customer confidence.
  • Start a customer list from Day 1: collect names, phone numbers, and WhatsApp contacts of every buyer.
  • Give your business a consistent name and logo and use it everywhere - consistency builds brand recognition over time.

📝 Practice Assignment

Create a simple marketing plan for your real or imaginary business. Include: (1) Create or improve a Facebook or WhatsApp Business profile with your business name, photo, and description. (2) Write 3 social media posts you will use this week (one product photo post, one customer testimonial, one tip related to your business). (3) List 5 specific people, groups, or organizations you will approach for referrals or partnerships. Post at least one of your prepared posts this week.
