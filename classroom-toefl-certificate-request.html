TOEFL Certificate Request

Request Your TOEFL Completion Certificate

Students who successfully complete the TOEFL Mock Examination can request their Certificate of Completion from Tolbert Innovation Hub.

To process the certificate, a certificate preparation fee of $5 USD is required.

Please complete the form below so we can verify your mock exam results and prepare your certificate.

Certificates can be:

  • Sent to you by email
  • Collected in person at the Tolbert Innovation Hub office

Processing time: 24–48 hours after payment and verification.

Payment Instructions

Certificate Processing Fee: $5 USD

Payment Options

  • Mobile Money
  • Bank Transfer
  • Office Payment

After making payment, please include your transaction ID in the form below.

Certificate Request Form

Complete and submit the form below. Ensure your payment transaction ID is ready before submitting.

Processing time: 24–48 hours after payment verification.

Certificate Processing Workflow

Step 1

Check the student's mock exam result.

Step 2

Confirm payment of the $5 certificate fee.

Step 3

Prepare the certificate.

Step 4

Send certificate by email or print for office pickup.

Certificate Design Information

  • Student Full Name
  • Course Title: TOEFL Preparation Program
  • Institution: Tolbert Innovation Hub
  • Completion Date
  • Certificate ID
  • Official Signature / Seal
