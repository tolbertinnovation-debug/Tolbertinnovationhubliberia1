📖 About This Course

In today's digital world, computer literacy is no longer optional — it is the foundation of every professional career. Whether you are a student, a job seeker, a small business owner, or someone who simply wants to keep up with the modern world, this course gives you everything you need to use a computer confidently, professionally, and safely.

This course was designed specifically for learners in Liberia and across Africa who may be encountering computers, smartphones, and digital tools for the first time. Instructor Samuel Tolbert — founder of Tolbert Innovation Hub — built this curriculum from years of teaching technology to beginners in Monrovia, understanding the exact gaps and challenges African learners face. Every lesson is practical, step-by-step, and immediately applicable to real life.

By the end of this course, you will be able to set up a professional email, write a CV in Microsoft Word, use Excel for basic data tracking, navigate the web safely, and present your ideas in PowerPoint. You will also earn a digitally verifiable TIH Academy certificate to showcase your new skills to employers, universities, and clients.
