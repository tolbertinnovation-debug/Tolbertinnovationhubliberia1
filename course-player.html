2.1 Using the Internet Safely
⏱ 7:15 📶 Module 2 · Lesson 5 of 16

In this lesson, you will learn how to navigate the internet safely and responsibly. We cover the difference between secure (HTTPS) and unsecure websites, how to recognize phishing emails and scams, and best practices for creating strong passwords that protect your personal information online.

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to identify trustworthy websites, safely conduct online transactions, and use two-factor authentication to add an extra layer of security to your accounts. These skills are essential for anyone who uses the internet for banking, communication, job searching, or running a business in today's digital world.

Is it safe to use public Wi-Fi for internet banking?
