Ways to Give

Choose Your Level of Support

Whether you can contribute a little or a lot, every amount creates meaningful impact. Below are our three giving tiers, each one targeted to a specific aspect of our work. All donations are coordinated directly through our team via WhatsApp or email.

Supporter
Any Amount / month

Every contribution at the Supporter level goes directly toward maintaining and expanding our free classroom resources, keeping the platform online, updating course materials, and producing new study content for IELTS and TOEFL students who cannot afford paid preparation programs.

Your Impact
Funds classroom resources for students who cannot afford paid prep
Donate as Supporter
Most Impactful
Champion
Monthly Support / ongoing

Champions directly sponsor an individual student's IELTS or TOEFL exam preparation journey. This tier covers the cost of study materials, mock exam resources, and access to tutor-led review sessions for one student per month, giving them the best possible chance of achieving their target score and qualifying for the scholarships they deserve.

Your Impact
Sponsors one student's full exam preparation journey each month
Donate as Champion
Transformer
Partnership Level / discuss with us

Transformer-level donors support the full arc of a student's scholarship journey, from initial eligibility assessment through application preparation, exam guidance, document coordination, and post-placement follow-up. This tier also contributes to healthcare referral coordination and technology program delivery for businesses and entrepreneurs.

Your Impact
Supports a complete scholarship journey from start to placement

Transparency

How Donations Are Used

We are committed to responsible, transparent stewardship of every contribution. Here is how donations are allocated across our four program categories.

📚
Classroom Resources

Maintaining and expanding free IELTS and TOEFL course content, study materials, and practice test systems for students across Liberia.

🎓
Scholarship Guidance

Supporting students through eligibility assessments, application preparation, document coordination, and admission letter processing.

🏥
Healthcare Support

Coordinating medical referrals, supporting families with documentation, and maintaining relationships with partner medical facilities abroad.

💻
Technology Programs

Delivering free and subsidized technology consultations to small businesses and entrepreneurs in Liberia who need digital support.

Organizations & Businesses

Corporate & Organizational Giving

We welcome partnerships with businesses, foundations, NGOs, and institutions that share our commitment to education, healthcare access, and technology empowerment in Liberia and Africa.

Why Partner With Tolbert Innovation Hub?

Corporate giving and organizational partnerships create a direct, traceable line between your resources and life-changing outcomes for young Liberians. Whether you want to sponsor a scholarship cohort, fund a specific program, or provide technology resources, we can build a partnership structure that works for your organization's goals and reporting requirements.
