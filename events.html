2026 Scholarship Application Deadline

The deadline for submitting your scholarship application for the 2026 intake is July 15, 2026. This applies to students applying for the ICCR Scholarship (India), Erasmus+ pathways (Europe), and other bilateral scholarship programs coordinated through Tolbert Innovation Hub.

To be considered, you must have completed your eligibility assessment, submitted all required documents, and received confirmation from our team. Applications submitted after this date will be reviewed for the next intake cycle. Do not wait — scholarship slots are limited.

What We've Done

Past Program Highlights

A look back at some of the events and program sessions that have shaped our community over the past year.

March 2026
IELTS Module Launch — All 6 Modules Released
Full six-module IELTS curriculum went live on the free classroom platform, covering Reading, Writing, Listening, Speaking, and exam strategy.
February 2026
Healthcare Referral Program Expansion
Expanded healthcare referral network to include facilities in Ghana and the UAE, increasing access for complex medical cases.
January 2026
Scholarship Intake Opening — 2026 Cycle
Opened applications for the 2026 scholarship cycle, including ICCR and Erasmus+ pathways, with over 200 initial inquiries received in the first week.
November 2025
Technology Consultation Drive — 15 Businesses Supported
A three-week consultation drive provided free digital assessments to 15 small businesses across Monrovia, resulting in 8 active technology projects.
October 2025
TOEFL Classroom Launch
The complete TOEFL preparation classroom launched with six modules, full mock exams, video lessons, and a certificate request system.
September 2025
First Cohort Scholarship Placements
Celebrated the first cohort of students successfully placed at international universities through our scholarship program guidance pathway.
