Scholarships

Who is eligible for the TIH scholarship program? +

The scholarship program is open to Liberian students and recent graduates with valid identification, academic transcripts from recognized institutions, and a personal statement explaining their study goals.

What fully funded scholarships are available for Liberian students? +

Several prestigious fully funded scholarships are open to Liberian students, including Chevening (UK), Fulbright (USA), Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program, DAAD (Germany), Pan African University, and UCL Global Undergraduate Scholarship. Each covers full tuition, living stipend, and more.

Learn more on our Fully Funded Scholarships page.

How do I apply for a scholarship? +

Visit our Scholarship Program page and complete the online application form. Our team will review your submission and contact you with next steps within 5–7 business days.

What documents do I need for the scholarship application? +

You'll need academic transcripts/certificates, valid national identification, a personal statement, and contact details. Specific requirements may vary by scholarship type. Check the relevant program page for the exact checklist.

Is there an application fee? +

Tolbert Innovation Hub's scholarship guidance service is 100% free. Some external scholarship programs may have their own fees. Check the specific scholarship's official page for the most current information.

When is the scholarship application deadline? +

Scholarship deadlines vary by program. Chevening opens August–November, Fulbright opens February–May, and Mastercard Foundation varies by partner university. Apply early for the best chance of consideration. Visit our Fully Funded Scholarships page for current deadlines.

What countries can I study in? +

Current scholarship pathways include India, Cyprus, United Kingdom, Canada, and the United States of America. We continue to expand our scholarship network. Check our programs page for the latest available pathways.

How long does the review process take? +

Applications are typically reviewed within 5–10 business days. Complete, accurate applications move through the process faster. Ensure all required documents are submitted to avoid delays.

Healthcare

What healthcare services does TIH provide? +

TIH facilitates medical case intake, specialist referrals, hospital coordination, and travel guidance for individuals seeking healthcare abroad, primarily in India. We act as a bridge between patients and international medical institutions.

How does TIH coordinate healthcare access? +

You submit a healthcare inquiry form, our team assesses your case, we coordinate with partner hospitals and specialists, and provide you with treatment guidance and travel support information. Visit our Healthcare page to begin the process.

Does TIH cover medical costs? +

No. TIH facilitates access and coordination. Medical costs are determined by the treating institutions. We help you understand costs, navigate the process, and connect you with the right resources to make informed decisions about your care.

How quickly can I get a healthcare referral? +

Initial assessment is usually within 3–5 business days. For urgent cases, contact us directly via WhatsApp at +231 880 559 227 for a faster response.

What medical specialties can TIH help with? +

We work with a wide network including oncology, cardiology, orthopedics, neurology, and general surgery specialists. Contact us with your specific case for accurate guidance on which partner institutions can best serve your needs.

IELTS / TOEFL Classroom

Is the classroom really free? +

Yes, 100% free. No payment, no registration required for access. Simply visit our Classroom page and choose your pathway (IELTS or TOEFL) to get started immediately.

What does the IELTS classroom include? +

6 structured modules covering Foundation, Listening & Reading, Writing & Speaking, Strategy & Techniques, Practice Tests, and Full Mock Exams, plus a certificate request system for students who complete all modules.

What's the difference between IELTS and TOEFL? +

IELTS (British Council) uses a Band Score 1–9 system and is widely accepted in the UK, Australia, Canada, and many universities globally.

TOEFL (ETS) uses a 0–120 score system and is preferred by many US universities. Both are internationally recognized. Your choice depends on where you're applying. Our classroom covers both pathways in full.

How do I get a certificate after completing the course? +

After completing the mock exams, use the Certificate Request form to request your completion certificate: IELTS Certificate Request or TOEFL Certificate Request.

Do I need an account to use the classroom? +

No account is needed for classroom access. All modules, quizzes, and resources are freely available to everyone.

How long does it take to complete the course? +

Most students complete each 6-module pathway in 4–8 weeks depending on study pace. You can go at your own speed. The classroom is available 24/7, so you can study whenever it suits you best.

Technology & Software

What software services does TIH offer? +

Business websites, web applications, custom software, school and business management systems, process automation, and digital transformation consulting. Visit our Software Platform page to see the full range of services.

How do I request a software consultation? +

Visit our Software Platform page and complete the Business Project Intake Form. We'll respond within 2–3 business days with consultation details and next steps for your project.

How long does a typical project take? +

Project timelines vary based on scope. A basic business website typically takes 2–4 weeks. Custom software and management systems may take 4–12 weeks. We'll give you a clear, detailed timeline in your project proposal before any work begins.

Does TIH work with businesses outside Liberia? +

Yes, we support organizations across Africa and beyond. All of our software and digital services can be delivered remotely. Contact us to discuss your project regardless of your location.
