Opportunities for Liberian Students

Fully Funded Scholarships You Can Apply For

Liberian students are eligible for several prestigious fully funded international scholarships. Explore each opportunity below. All cover tuition, living costs, and more.

🇬🇧

United Kingdom

Chevening Scholarship

Master's Degree Fully Funded Liberia Eligible

The UK Government's flagship international scholarship program, funded by the Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office. One full year of Master's study at any UK university.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition fees
  • Monthly living allowance
  • Return economy airfare
  • Arrival & departure allowance
  • Thesis / dissertation grant

Key Requirements

  • Liberian citizen
  • Bachelor's degree + 2 years work experience
  • Strong English proficiency
  • Commitment to return to Liberia after study
📅 Applications open: Aug – Nov each year Apply / Learn More →
🇺🇸

United States of America

Fulbright Foreign Student Program

Master's / PhD Fully Funded Liberia Eligible

The U.S. Government's premier international exchange program, enabling graduate students from Liberia to pursue master's degrees and research in the United States. ~4,000 awards granted annually worldwide.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition and university fees
  • Monthly stipend for living expenses
  • International airfare
  • Health insurance
  • Research / professional development funds

Key Requirements

  • Liberian citizenship (must reside in Liberia)
  • Bachelor's degree with strong academic record
  • Fluent English (TOEFL iBT ≥ 79 or IELTS ≥ 6.5)
  • Leadership potential and community commitment
📅 Deadline: Varies by country (Feb – May) Apply / Learn More →

Africa & Global Universities

Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program

Undergraduate & Postgraduate Fully Funded Open to Africa

One of Africa's largest scholarship programs, partnering with 40+ leading universities across Africa and globally. Focuses on young Africans from economically disadvantaged backgrounds who demonstrate leadership potential.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition and university fees
  • Accommodation and living stipend
  • Mentorship and leadership development
  • Return travel allowance
  • Health insurance (at select institutions)

Key Requirements

  • African citizenship (including Liberia)
  • Financial need demonstrated
  • Demonstrated leadership & community service
  • Commitment to give back to Africa
📅 Varies by partner university Apply / Learn More →
🇩🇪

Germany / Sub-Saharan Africa

DAAD In-Country / In-Region Programme

Master's / PhD Fully Funded Sub-Saharan Africa

Funded by Germany's Federal Ministry for Economic Cooperation (BMZ), this program supports graduates from Sub-Saharan Africa, including Liberia, to pursue Master's degrees within their home region in Africa.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition fees
  • Monthly stipend up to €8,920/year
  • Health insurance
  • Study and research allowance
  • Travel subsidy

Key Requirements

  • Sub-Saharan African citizenship (Liberia eligible)
  • Bachelor's degree (first academic degree)
  • Max. 6 years post-graduation at time of application
  • English or French proficiency
📅 New calls published: autumn each year Apply / Learn More →

African Union, Pan-Africa

Pan African University (PAU) Scholarship

Master's / PhD Fully Funded All AU Member States

An initiative of the African Union Heads of State, the Pan African University network provides full scholarships for African students to pursue postgraduate studies at five thematic institutes across the continent, promoting African integration and excellence.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition fees
  • Monthly living stipend
  • Accommodation on campus
  • Return air ticket
  • Medical insurance

Key Requirements

  • African Union member state citizen (Liberia ✓)
  • Bachelor's degree with strong GPA
  • Under 35 years of age (Master's); under 40 (PhD)
  • English proficiency required
📅 Applications open: Nov – Dec each year Apply / Learn More →

United Kingdom (UCL)

UCL Global Undergraduate Scholarship

Undergraduate Fully Funded Low-Income Countries

University College London (UCL), ranked top 10 globally, offers this scholarship to enable students from low-income backgrounds, including Liberia, to pursue full-time undergraduate degrees at one of the world's leading universities.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition fees
  • Annual living allowance (London)
  • Return airfare contribution
  • Mentoring and career support

Key Requirements

  • Citizen of a low or lower-middle income country (Liberia ✓)
  • Outstanding academic achievement (senior secondary)
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Strong English proficiency
📅 Deadline: Jan – Mar each year Apply / Learn More →
🇺🇸

United States

SHE-CAN Scholarship

Undergraduate Full Ride Women Only Liberia Eligible

SHE-CAN (She Can, I Can) provides a full scholarship for young Liberian women to study at top US colleges. The program invests in future female leaders who commit to returning home and serving their country.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition and university fees
  • Housing and meals
  • Test preparation (SAT / TOEFL)
  • Passport and visa costs
  • Round-trip flights
  • Textbooks and health insurance

Key Requirements

  • Liberian female student, ages 17–20
  • Strong academic record
  • Commitment to return to Liberia within 1 year of graduation
  • Commitment to serve in Liberia for at least 3 years
📅 Applications open annually; check website Apply / Learn More →
🇪🇺

European Union

Erasmus Mundus Scholarship

Master's Degree Fully Funded Open to All Nationalities

Erasmus Mundus Joint Master's Degrees are prestigious EU-funded programs that allow students to study at two or more European universities. Liberian students are highly encouraged to apply as the EU prioritizes students from partner countries.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition at all partner universities
  • Monthly living allowance of €1,300
  • Travel and installation allowances
  • Health insurance

Key Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in a relevant field
  • Open to all nationalities (Liberia ✓)
  • Strong academic performance
  • English or other language proficiency (varies by program)
📅 Applications: Oct – Jan each year Apply / Learn More →
🇰🇷

South Korea

Global Korea Scholarship (GKS/KGSP)

Undergraduate & Master's Fully Funded Liberia Eligible

The Global Korea Scholarship (formerly KGSP), funded by the Korean Government, offers full scholarships for international students to study at Korean universities at both undergraduate and graduate levels. It includes Korean language training before studies begin.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition and enrollment fees
  • Monthly stipend (KRW 900,000–1,000,000)
  • Round-trip airfare
  • 1-year Korean language training
  • Medical insurance
  • Settlement and research allowances

Key Requirements

  • Citizen of a GKS partner country (Liberia ✓)
  • Under 25 (undergraduate) or under 40 (graduate) years of age
  • GPA of 2.64 / 4.0 or higher
  • Good health (medical examination required)
📅 Applications: Feb – Mar each year Apply / Learn More →
🇯🇵

Japan

MEXT Japan Scholarship

Undergraduate & Postgraduate Fully Funded Liberia Eligible

The Japanese Government MEXT Scholarship is one of the most prestigious scholarships in Asia, covering studies at Japanese universities for both undergraduate and graduate students. Liberian students apply through the Embassy of Japan (accredited via Ghana).

What's Covered

  • Full tuition and examination fees
  • Monthly living allowance (¥117,000–145,000)
  • Round-trip airfare
  • Japanese language training before enrollment

Key Requirements

  • Liberian citizen aged 17–24 (undergraduate) or under 35 (graduate)
  • Strong academic record
  • Apply through Embassy of Japan (accredited to Liberia via Ghana)
  • Medical fitness certificate required
📅 Applications: Apr – Jun each year (Embassy route) Apply / Learn More →

United Kingdom – Commonwealth

Commonwealth Scholarship

Master's & PhD Fully Funded Distance Learning Option Liberia Eligible

Commonwealth Scholarships are funded by the UK Government for citizens of Commonwealth countries including Liberia. They offer fully-funded Master's and PhD opportunities at top UK universities, plus a Distance Learning track for those who prefer to study from home.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition and university fees
  • Monthly stipend and living allowance
  • Return airfare to the UK
  • Thesis grant and study travel allowance
  • Distance learning option available (no relocation needed)

Key Requirements

  • Citizen of a Commonwealth country (Liberia ✓)
  • Bachelor's degree (2:1 or equivalent) for Master's
  • Commitment to development in your home country
  • Strong English proficiency
📅 Applications: Aug – Oct each year Apply / Learn More →
🇹🇷

Turkey

Türkiye Burslari (Turkish Government Scholarship)

Undergraduate & Postgraduate Fully Funded Liberia Eligible

The Turkish Government Scholarship (Türkiye Burslari) is one of the most popular scholarships for African students, covering undergraduate, Master's, and PhD degrees at top Turkish universities. It includes free Turkish language training before studies begin.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition at a Turkish university
  • Monthly stipend (varies by level: UG/MA/PhD)
  • Free on-campus accommodation
  • Round-trip airfare
  • One year Turkish language course (free)
  • Health insurance

Key Requirements

  • Citizen of a non-Turkish country (Liberia ✓)
  • Age: under 21 (UG), under 30 (Master's), under 35 (PhD)
  • Strong academic record
  • Online application through official portal
📅 Applications: Jan – Feb each year Apply / Learn More →
🇨🇳

China

Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC)

Undergraduate, Master's & PhD Fully Funded Liberia Eligible

The Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) is one of the largest scholarship programs in the world, offering thousands of spots annually to African students for study at top Chinese universities. Many programs are available in English.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition at a Chinese university
  • Monthly living allowance
  • Free on-campus dormitory accommodation
  • Comprehensive medical insurance
  • One-time settlement and travel allowance

Key Requirements

  • Citizen of a country with diplomatic relations with China (Liberia ✓)
  • Good health and academic record
  • Age: under 25 (UG), under 35 (MA), under 40 (PhD)
  • Apply via Chinese Embassy or university directly
📅 Applications: Jan – Apr each year Apply / Learn More →
🇦🇺

Australia

Australia Awards Scholarship

Undergraduate & Master's Fully Funded Africa Eligible

Australia Awards are prestigious international scholarships funded by the Australian Government (DFAT) for students from developing countries in Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. They aim to develop leadership capacity and lasting development outcomes.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition and university fees
  • Return economy airfare
  • Establishment and living allowances
  • Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC)
  • Pre-course English language training (if needed)

Key Requirements

  • Citizen of an eligible developing country (check eligibility)
  • Minimum 2 years work experience (for most programs)
  • Commitment to return home after study
  • Strong English proficiency
📅 Applications: Feb – Apr each year (varies by country) Apply / Learn More →

United Kingdom

Gates Cambridge Scholarship

Master's & PhD Fully Funded Highly Competitive Liberia Eligible

The Gates Cambridge Scholarship is one of the world's most prestigious awards, funded by the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation. It funds outstanding students from outside the UK to study any subject at the University of Cambridge, one of the top 3 universities in the world.

What's Covered

  • Full University of Cambridge tuition
  • Maintenance allowance (£21,000+/year)
  • Return airfare
  • Family allowance (if applicable)
  • Academic development funding

Key Requirements

  • Citizen of any country outside the UK (Liberia ✓)
  • Exceptional academic achievement
  • Apply to Cambridge and Gates simultaneously
  • Demonstrated commitment to improving lives of others
📅 Applications: Oct – Dec each year Apply / Learn More →

China

Schwarzman Scholars Program

Master's Degree Fully Funded Leadership Focus Liberia Eligible

Schwarzman Scholars is a highly competitive 1-year Master's program at Tsinghua University in Beijing, designed to prepare the next generation of global leaders. It is modelled after Oxford's Rhodes Scholarship and fully funded by the Schwarzman Foundation.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition at Tsinghua University, Beijing
  • Room and board on campus
  • Travel to and from China
  • Stipend and in-country travel for study trips
  • Laptop and health insurance

Key Requirements

  • Any nationality (Liberia ✓)
  • Age 18–28 at time of enrollment
  • Bachelor's degree or final year of undergraduate study
  • Demonstrated leadership potential
📅 Applications: Sep – Oct each year Apply / Learn More →

Islamic Development Bank

IsDB Scholarship Program

Undergraduate, Master's & PhD Fully Funded Muslim Students Liberia Eligible

The Islamic Development Bank (IsDB) Scholarship Program supports Muslim students from member countries, including Liberia, to pursue undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD degrees at reputable international universities, with a focus on STEM, health, and development fields.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition at approved university
  • Monthly living allowance
  • Airfare (once per academic year)
  • Health insurance
  • Book and research allowances

Key Requirements

  • Muslim student from an IsDB member country (Liberia ✓)
  • Age under 35 (postgraduate), under 24 (undergraduate)
  • Strong academic record
  • Commitment to return and contribute to home country
📅 Applications: Jan – Mar each year Apply / Learn More →
🕊️

Global: Rotary International

Rotary Peace Fellowship

Master's & Professional Certificate Fully Funded Peace & Development Focus Open to All Nationalities

The Rotary Peace Fellowship funds professionals dedicated to peace and international understanding for Master's degrees or professional development certificates at one of seven Rotary Peace Centers worldwide, including universities in the UK, USA, Japan, Australia, Sweden, Uganda, and Thailand.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition and fees at a Rotary Peace Center university
  • Room and board
  • Round-trip transportation
  • Internship and field study funding

Key Requirements

  • Open to all nationalities (Liberia ✓)
  • Minimum 3 years of relevant work/volunteer experience
  • Bachelor's degree (for Master's track)
  • Demonstrated commitment to peace and cross-cultural understanding
📅 Applications: May – Aug each year Apply / Learn More →
