Opportunities for Liberian Students

Fully Funded Scholarships You Can Apply For

Liberian students are eligible for several prestigious fully funded international scholarships. Explore each opportunity below — all cover tuition, living costs, and more.

🇬🇧

United Kingdom

Chevening Scholarship

Master's Degree Fully Funded Liberia Eligible

The UK Government's flagship international scholarship programme, funded by the Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office. One full year of Master's study at any UK university.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition fees
  • Monthly living allowance
  • Return economy airfare
  • Arrival & departure allowance
  • Thesis / dissertation grant

Key Requirements

  • Liberian citizen
  • Bachelor's degree + 2 years work experience
  • Strong English proficiency
  • Commitment to return to Liberia after study
📅 Applications open: Aug – Nov each year Apply / Learn More →
🇺🇸

United States of America

Fulbright Foreign Student Program

Master's / PhD Fully Funded Liberia Eligible

The U.S. Government's premier international exchange program, enabling graduate students from Liberia to pursue master's degrees and research in the United States. ~4,000 awards granted annually worldwide.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition and university fees
  • Monthly stipend for living expenses
  • International airfare
  • Health insurance
  • Research / professional development funds

Key Requirements

  • Liberian citizenship (must reside in Liberia)
  • Bachelor's degree with strong academic record
  • Fluent English (TOEFL iBT ≥ 79 or IELTS ≥ 6.5)
  • Leadership potential and community commitment
📅 Deadline: Varies by country (Feb – May) Apply / Learn More →

Africa & Global Universities

Mastercard Foundation Scholars Program

Undergraduate & Postgraduate Fully Funded Open to Africa

One of Africa's largest scholarship programs, partnering with 40+ leading universities across Africa and globally. Focuses on young Africans from economically disadvantaged backgrounds who demonstrate leadership potential.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition and university fees
  • Accommodation and living stipend
  • Mentorship and leadership development
  • Return travel allowance
  • Health insurance (at select institutions)

Key Requirements

  • African citizenship (including Liberia)
  • Financial need demonstrated
  • Demonstrated leadership & community service
  • Commitment to give back to Africa
📅 Varies by partner university Apply / Learn More →
🇩🇪

Germany / Sub-Saharan Africa

DAAD In-Country / In-Region Programme

Master's / PhD Fully Funded Sub-Saharan Africa

Funded by Germany's Federal Ministry for Economic Cooperation (BMZ), this program supports graduates from Sub-Saharan Africa — including Liberia — to pursue Master's degrees within their home region in Africa.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition fees
  • Monthly stipend up to €8,920/year
  • Health insurance
  • Study and research allowance
  • Travel subsidy

Key Requirements

  • Sub-Saharan African citizenship (Liberia eligible)
  • Bachelor's degree (first academic degree)
  • Max. 6 years post-graduation at time of application
  • English or French proficiency
📅 New calls published: autumn each year Apply / Learn More →
🌐

African Union — Pan-Africa

Pan African University (PAU) Scholarship

Master's / PhD Fully Funded All AU Member States

An initiative of the African Union Heads of State, the Pan African University network provides full scholarships for African students to pursue postgraduate studies at five thematic institutes across the continent, promoting African integration and excellence.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition fees
  • Monthly living stipend
  • Accommodation on campus
  • Return air ticket
  • Medical insurance

Key Requirements

  • African Union member state citizen (Liberia ✓)
  • Bachelor's degree with strong GPA
  • Under 35 years of age (Master's); under 40 (PhD)
  • English proficiency required
📅 Applications open: Nov – Dec each year Apply / Learn More →
🏛️

United Kingdom — UCL

UCL Global Undergraduate Scholarship

Undergraduate Fully Funded Low-Income Countries

University College London (UCL) — ranked top 10 globally — offers this scholarship to enable students from low-income backgrounds, including Liberia, to pursue full-time undergraduate degrees at one of the world's leading universities.

What's Covered

  • Full tuition fees
  • Annual living allowance (London)
  • Return airfare contribution
  • Mentoring and career support

Key Requirements

  • Citizen of a low or lower-middle income country (Liberia ✓)
  • Outstanding academic achievement (senior secondary)
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Strong English proficiency
📅 Deadline: Jan – Mar each year Apply / Learn More →
