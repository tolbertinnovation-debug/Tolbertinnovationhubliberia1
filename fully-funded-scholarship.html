ICCR (Indian Council for Cultural Relations) Scholarship for the 2026–2027 academic year.

This scholarship is offered by the Government of India under the India-Africa Maitri initiative, providing fully funded opportunities for Liberian students to pursue Undergraduate, Postgraduate, or PhD studies in India.

The ICCR Scholarship aims to foster cultural relations and mutual understanding between India and other nations. By offering fully funded opportunities, the Indian government encourages international students to explore the diverse educational landscape of India.

Important Notes

  • Only complete, accurate applications will be considered.
  • Incomplete or duplicate submissions will be disqualified.
  • Applicants must check course eligibility and availability with their preferred universities.
  • A non-refundable application fee of $100 USD must be paid by 30 April 2026.
  • The completed application must be submitted by 21 April 2026.

If you need help or have questions, contact: Tolbert Innovation Hub, Spriggs Payne Airport, Sinkor, Monrovia

Phone/WhatsApp: +231 778 956 979 / 0880 559 227

Scholarship Overview

About the ICCR Scholarship: This scholarship covers:

  • Full tuition fees (paid directly to the university)
  • Monthly living stipend
  • Return airfare (economy-class)
  • Visa support through the Indian Mission
  • Hostel accommodation (subject to availability)

Note: Personal medical insurance is required and must be arranged by the applicant.

Eligibility

  • Liberian citizenship
  • Age 18–40 (UG/PG), up to 50 (PhD)
  • Proficiency in English (through essay or TOEFL/IELTS/DUOLINGO scores)
  • Strong academic background
