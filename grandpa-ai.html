The Problem

Millions of Africans Are Left Behind by Global AI

Most AI tools are built in English and other global languages, leaving millions of Liberians and Africans unable to fully benefit from digital innovation. Cultural knowledge (oral histories, traditions, indigenous wisdom) is disappearing as it is never captured in digital formats.

  • AI assistants don't understand Liberian English or local dialects
  • Cultural knowledge and oral traditions are rapidly disappearing
  • Students, farmers, and entrepreneurs lack AI tools they can use
  • Language barriers block access to education, health, and business information
  • Africa's unique heritage is not represented in global AI training data
Grandpa AI solves language exclusion and cultural knowledge loss by giving Africans, especially Liberians, AI access in local languages, preserving traditions, and expanding education.
, Samuel S. Tolbert, Founder & CEO, Tolbert Innovation Hub Liberia

What Grandpa AI Does

A Culturally Intelligent AI for Every Liberian

Grandpa AI carries the wisdom of the village elder (patient, knowledgeable, trustworthy) combined with the power of modern artificial intelligence.

🗣️

Speaks Your Language

Communicates in simple Liberian English, colloquial speech, and will support indigenous Liberian languages. AI that understands how you actually talk.

📖

Preserves Cultural Heritage

Stores and shares African stories, oral histories, traditions, and values. Every conversation keeps our culture alive for younger generations.

🎓

Education Support

Helps students with homework, explains concepts in simple terms, and provides WAEC and school preparation support in a familiar, friendly way.

🌾

Agriculture & Livelihoods

Guides farmers on crop care, weather patterns, market prices, and best practices in a language they understand and trust.

💼

Business Training

Provides practical business advice: from writing a business plan to pricing products and accessing grants for entrepreneurs and market traders.

🏥

Health Awareness

Answers basic health questions, explains symptoms, promotes preventive care, and guides users to appropriate services in their community.

💡

Digital Literacy

Teaches people how to use smartphones, the internet, and digital tools in a step-by-step, judgment-free way that builds real digital confidence.

🌍

Pan-African Vision

Designed to scale across Africa, adapting to local languages and cultures in each country. Built in Liberia, built for the continent.

Who Grandpa AI Serves

Built for Every Liberian Community

From the classroom to the farm, from market stalls to healthcare clinics, Grandpa AI meets people where they are.

📚 Students & Youth

Get homework help, WAEC prep, scholarship guidance, and career advice in plain Liberian English. No judgment, no barriers; just answers when you need them.

🌾 Farmers & Agricultural Workers

Ask about planting seasons, pest control, fertilizers, and how to access markets, all without needing to read complex agricultural manuals.

🛍️ Market Women & Entrepreneurs

Get business tips, pricing strategies, record-keeping help, and information about grants and loans available to small businesses in Liberia.

🏘️ Community Leaders & Elders

Preserve your community's stories, traditions, and oral history. Grandpa AI helps record and protect cultural knowledge for future generations.

🏥 Health Workers & Citizens

Provide community health education, symptom awareness, and referral guidance in simple language that everyone can understand and act on.

🏢 NGOs & Development Organizations

Deploy Grandpa AI as a field tool for community outreach, data collection, and information sharing without requiring technical expertise from staff.
