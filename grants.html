EU Spotlight Initiative: Women & Girls Protection

NGO / CSO Women Signed March 2026

💰Amount:$13.6 million total program
🎯Focus:Gender-based violence, women empowerment, policy reform
✅Eligible:Registered NGOs, CSOs, women's organizations

Youth Entrepreneurship Investment Bank (YEIB)

Youth Business / MSME Agriculture Digital Operations 2026

💰Amount:$16M total fund — individual grants vary
🎯Focus:Agribusiness, digital services, mining, tourism

Business / MSME Youth Active 2026

🎯Focus:Business growth, entrepreneurship, sustainable scaling

Tony Elumelu Foundation (TEF)

TEF Entrepreneurship Programme 2026

Business Youth Jan – Mar 2026

The Tony Elumelu Foundation 2026 Entrepreneurship Programme offers $5,000 non-refundable seed capital, 12 weeks of business training, and mentorship to entrepreneurs across all 54 African countries, including Liberia. Since 2015, TEF has funded 24,000+ entrepreneurs generating $4.2B+ in revenue.

💰Amount:$5,000 non-refundable seed capital + mentorship
🎯Focus:Any sector — business idea or early-stage enterprise
✅Eligible:African citizen, age 18+, business 0–3 years old
📅Cycle:Annual — Jan 1 to Mar 1 each year on TEFConnect.com
Apply at TEF ↗ Get TIH Help $5,000 Seed
