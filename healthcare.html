International Healthcare Exposure Program

Comprehensive Healthcare Support for Liberian Families

This program provides structured support for individuals seeking specialized healthcare access opportunities, especially through partner options in India.

What We Support

  • Initial medical case intake and record review
  • Hospital and specialist referral coordination
  • Estimated treatment planning guidance
  • Travel, appointment, and communication support

Program Flow

  1. Submit inquiry and medical background details.
  2. Our team performs a preliminary case assessment.
  3. We coordinate referrals with partner healthcare institutions.
  4. You receive next-step guidance for treatment preparation.

Important Note

Tolbert Innovation Hub facilitates access and coordination support. Final treatment decisions, costs, and timelines are determined by partner healthcare institutions.

We are committed to transparency and will provide you with all available information to make informed healthcare decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of medical cases does Tolbert Innovation Hub support?
We support a wide range of cases including cardiac conditions, cancer/oncology, neurological disorders, orthopedic issues, and complex surgeries that require specialist care not available locally in Liberia.
How long does the referral process take?
Initial case review takes 3–5 business days. Once a partner hospital confirms availability, we coordinate the appointment, typically within 2–4 weeks for elective cases and faster for urgent situations.
What are the costs involved?
Tolbert Innovation Hub charges a coordination fee for our services. Medical treatment costs are set by the partner hospitals and vary by condition, procedure, and facility. We provide a cost estimate before any commitment.
Does TIH help with visa applications for medical travel?
Yes. We assist with compiling the necessary documents for a medical visa application to India, including hospital invitation letters, case summaries, and guidance on the visa process.
What documents are required to start the referral process?
You will need: recent medical reports and test results, a referral letter from a local doctor (if available), a copy of your passport, and contact details for the primary caregiver or family liaison.
Can TIH support emergency medical cases?
For emergencies, contact us immediately via WhatsApp at +231 880 559 227. We have expedited processes for urgent cases and can provide faster coordination for life-threatening situations.
Does TIH accompany patients to India?
While TIH does not physically accompany patients, we provide detailed guidance on airport pickups, hospital arrival procedures, and maintain contact throughout your treatment journey.
Are there healthcare options outside of India?
Currently, our primary partner network is in India. We are expanding to include hospitals in other countries. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
