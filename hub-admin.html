💳 How Paid Access Works

Every course and WASSCE subject is locked. A learner pays US$2 by Mobile Money to 0880559227 · Samuel Tolbert, then sends you their payment confirmation with their Student ID. Once you confirm the payment arrived, generate their access code below and send it to them — they enter it to unlock that one course/subject.
