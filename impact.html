Community Impact

The Human Stories Behind Our Numbers

Data tells part of the story. The full story lives in the experiences of the people we serve.

Education & Scholarships

Before Tolbert Innovation Hub, many Liberian students with academic potential had no clear path to international education. They faced a maze of application requirements, document submissions, and language proficiency tests with no structured support. Our Scholarship Program has created a clear, step-by-step pathway, from eligibility assessment through to admission letter processing, that has helped hundreds of students turn their international education dreams into concrete plans with real deadlines and achievable milestones.

Healthcare & Medical Access

Liberia's healthcare system, while improving, still faces challenges in treating complex or specialized medical conditions. For families who discover a loved one needs a procedure or specialist care unavailable locally, the path to international treatment can feel impossible. Our Healthcare Exposure Program has stepped into that gap, coordinating referrals, helping families understand their options, and providing structured guidance through visa, travel, and medical documentation processes so that geography no longer determines medical outcomes.

Technology & Business Innovation

Liberia's entrepreneurs and small business owners are energetic, creative, and ambitious, but many lack access to the digital tools and technical expertise that businesses in larger economies take for granted. Our Software Innovation Program bridges this gap by providing affordable technology consultations, custom digital solutions, and practical training that help businesses establish a credible online presence, streamline operations, and reach new customers. Each business supported creates jobs, generates income, and demonstrates what is possible when Liberian enterprise meets modern technology.
