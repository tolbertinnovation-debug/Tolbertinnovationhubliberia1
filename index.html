Why Tolbert Innovation Hub

More Than Programs: A Movement for Change

Inspired by the legacy of President William R. Tolbert Jr., the 20th President of Liberia, remembered for his commitment to rural development, education, and lifting the standard of living for all Liberians, we carry that vision forward through technology, scholarships, and access to opportunity.

🎯

Mission-Driven
