Core Programs

Focused Pathways for Students, Patients & Businesses

Three flagship programs designed to create measurable opportunity and long-term impact across Liberia and Africa.

Scholarship Program

Supporting Liberian students with eligibility guidance, application assistance, and structured admission letter issuance for qualified candidates pursuing international education.

  • Eligibility assessment and document preparation
  • Step-by-step application workflow support
  • ICCR Fully Funded Scholarship (India-Africa Maitri)
  • Admission letter processing and issuance
Explore Scholarship Program

International Healthcare Exposure Program

Connecting individuals and families to quality medical opportunities abroad — from initial inquiry through to comprehensive treatment planning and travel support.

  • Healthcare access opportunities in India
  • Medical case intake and specialist referrals
  • Hospital coordination and travel guidance
  • Ongoing patient support services
Explore Healthcare Program

Software Development & Business Innovation

Helping Liberian businesses modernize operations through professional websites, custom software tools, and strategic digital transformation consulting.

  • Custom websites and web applications
  • Business management systems and platforms
  • Digital strategy and consultation
  • Process automation and optimization
