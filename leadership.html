About the Founder

Mr. Samuel S. Tolbert is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Tolbert Innovation Hub, an organization he established with a clear and urgent purpose: to create real, measurable opportunity for Liberian youth in education, healthcare, and technology.

Driven by the belief that every young Liberian deserves access to the same global opportunities available elsewhere, Mr. Tolbert built TIH from the ground up, starting with scholarship guidance and growing into a multi-program hub that serves students, patients, and businesses across Liberia and beyond.

His leadership is rooted in direct action: personally guiding scholarship applicants through complex admission processes, coordinating healthcare referrals for families in need, and building digital infrastructure for Liberian businesses to modernize and grow.

Under Mr. Tolbert's direction, TIH has supported over 500 students with scholarship guidance, referred patients to specialist hospitals in India, and delivered digital solutions to businesses across Liberia, all while maintaining a free IELTS and TOEFL classroom for every student who walks through TIH's doors.

Our Story

How Tolbert Innovation Hub Began

From a bold idea to a multi-program organization serving thousands of Liberians: the TIH journey.

The Vision

A Problem Worth Solving

Mr. Samuel S. Tolbert observed firsthand that talented Liberian students were losing scholarship opportunities due to lack of guidance, and families were struggling to access quality healthcare abroad. He decided to build the bridge that was missing.

The Foundation

Tolbert Innovation Hub Is Founded

Mr. Tolbert formally established Tolbert Innovation Hub in Liberia, West Africa, with a mandate to serve students, patients, and businesses through dedicated scholarship guidance, healthcare coordination, and digital innovation programs.

Early Growth

Scholarship & Healthcare Programs Launch

TIH began processing its first scholarship applications and healthcare referrals, partnering with institutions in India and supporting Liberian students with fully funded scholarship guidance and international university admissions.

Expansion

Free IELTS & TOEFL Classroom Opens

Recognizing that exam preparation was a barrier for many students, Mr. Tolbert launched TIH's free IELTS and TOEFL classroom: 6 structured modules, practice tests, and a certificate program available to every student at no cost.

2026

Digital Innovation & Full Platform Launch

TIH launched its full digital platform: study abroad portal, software innovation services, student portal, and the chatbot assistant, expanding its reach across Liberia and serving 500+ students with scholarship guidance, healthcare referrals, and business digital solutions.

The Three Pillars

Built on Three Foundations

Mr. Tolbert designed TIH around three pillars that reflect the most urgent needs he observed in Liberian communities.

🎓

Education & Scholarship Access

Providing every Liberian student with the guidance, documentation support, and exam preparation tools needed to qualify for and secure international scholarships and university admissions.

🏥

International Healthcare Access

Connecting families to life-changing medical treatment abroad: coordinating referrals, hospital partnerships, and patient support from Liberia to specialist centers in India and beyond.

💻

Digital Innovation & Technology

Helping Liberian businesses modernize through custom websites, business management platforms, and digital transformation consulting: building the infrastructure for a stronger Liberian economy.

📚

Free Exam Preparation

Running a 100% free IELTS and TOEFL classroom so that no student is disadvantaged by cost when preparing for the international exams required for scholarship eligibility and university admission.
