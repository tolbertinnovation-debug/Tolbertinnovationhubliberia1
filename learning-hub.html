How It Works

Quality over quantity, every learner is reviewed and personally onboarded by the TIH team.

1

Apply Online

Fill the free application form. It takes 5 minutes, tell us your goals and pick the tracks you want.

2

Admin Review

Our team reviews every application within 3–5 business days. Track your status anytime with your Application ID.

3

Pay & Unlock

Approved? Log in, then pay US$2 per course by Mobile Money. The TIH team confirms your payment and sends an access code to unlock it.

4

Learn & Get Certified

Complete lessons in order, pass the quizzes, then request your certificate, every certificate is reviewed and approved by the TIH team before it is issued.
