How We Can Work Together

Partnership Types

We collaborate with a wide range of organizations and individuals who share our commitment to education, health, technology, and community development across Africa.

🏢

Corporate Partners

CSR initiatives, employee skill development, technology sponsorship, and brand alignment with youth empowerment and education in Liberia.

🎓

Educational Institutions

Academic collaborations, course exchanges, joint research partnerships, and student pathway programs linking institutions globally to Liberian learners.

🌍

NGOs & International Organizations

Program co-implementation, joint funding applications, technical support, and community outreach partnerships aligned with sustainable development goals.

🏛️

Government Agencies

Policy support, program endorsement, capacity building partnerships, and alignment with national education and healthcare transformation priorities.

🏥

Healthcare Organizations

Medical referral networks, specialist access agreements, health education program sponsorship, and collaborative healthcare delivery partnerships.

💰

Investors & Sponsors

Financial support for scholarships, program infrastructure, classroom technology, and innovation initiatives that deliver measurable community impact.

Why Partner With TIH

Benefits of Partnership

Partnering with Tolbert Innovation Hub means your organization contributes directly to transformative impact while gaining meaningful benefits aligned with your strategic goals.

📣

Brand Visibility

Gain exposure to thousands of students, families, and businesses across Liberia and the African diaspora through our programs, website, and communications.

🌱

Social Impact

🌐

Network Access

Connect with TIH's growing network of students, educators, healthcare professionals, technology innovators, and community leaders across Liberia and Africa.

⚙️

Custom Programs

Collaborate on programs specifically tailored to your organization's strategic priorities, CSR mandates, and community development objectives.
