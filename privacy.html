Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information at Tolbert Innovation Hub.

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you submit scholarship applications, sign up for our classroom, or contact us — including your name, email address, phone number, and educational background.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to process scholarship applications, provide access to our free classroom programs, send program updates and communications, and improve our services for Liberian students and families.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with partner universities and healthcare providers solely for the purpose of fulfilling your application or referral request, with your consent.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. All form submissions use HTTPS encryption.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@tolbertinnovationhub.org or visit our Contact page.
