Welcome to Tolbert Innovation Hub ("TIH", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website at www.tolbertinnovationhub.org, use our programs, or contact our team. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a scholarship or program application (name, email, phone number, date of birth, county, educational background, personal statement)
  • Register for or use our free classroom (name, email, study goals)
  • Submit a healthcare inquiry (name, contact details, medical information you choose to share)
  • Request a software consultation (name, organization, project details)
  • Contact us via our website, email, or WhatsApp
  • Sign up for news updates or communications
  • Apply to volunteer or partner with us

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may collect certain technical data automatically, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search terms
  • IP address (used only for geographic analytics, not for personal identification)

Information Stored on Your Device

Our website uses localStorage (browser-based storage) to remember your preferences such as dark mode settings, classroom progress, cookie consent choices, and application drafts. This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Program delivery: Processing scholarship applications, coordinating healthcare referrals, providing access to classroom resources, and delivering software services
  • Communication: Responding to your inquiries, sending program updates, and notifying you of application status changes
  • Improvement: Analyzing how our website and programs are used to improve quality and user experience
  • Safety and compliance: Detecting, preventing, and addressing fraud or security issues
  • Legal obligations: Complying with applicable laws and regulations in Liberia

We will not use your information for purposes other than those listed above without your consent.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Partner universities and institutions: When you apply for a study abroad program, we share your application details with relevant partner universities in India, Cyprus, or other partner countries, with your knowledge and consent
  • Healthcare providers: When you submit a healthcare inquiry, we may share relevant medical details with partner hospitals or specialist referral networks to coordinate your care, with your consent
  • Form processing services: We use Formsubmit.co to route form submissions to our email inbox. Formsubmit.co processes your form data in transit according to its own privacy policy
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority in Liberia

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy, typically:

  • Application data: Up to 2 years from submission date, or until the application cycle closes
  • Healthcare inquiries: Up to 3 years for coordination and follow-up purposes
  • General inquiries and communications: Up to 1 year
  • Newsletter subscribers: Until you unsubscribe

After the retention period, your data is securely deleted or anonymized. Data stored in your browser (localStorage) remains on your device until you clear your browser storage. We have no control over this data.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption for all web traffic and form submissions
  • Restricting access to personal data to authorized team members only
  • Using reputable, established form processing services
  • Regularly reviewing our security practices

While we take these precautions, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your personal information confidential.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal tracking. We use a cookie consent banner that stores your preference in your browser's localStorage. We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. Our website may use Google Fonts, which may set its own cookies; we recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy for details.

You can withdraw your cookie consent at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for our website, or by using your browser's built-in privacy settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Objection: Object to how we process your information for specific purposes
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format where technically feasible

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@tolbertinnovationhub.org. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our programs are designed for individuals aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16 without verifiable parental or guardian consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete that information.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites including partner universities, scholarship programs, healthcare institutions, and external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any website you visit through links from our platform.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or services. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users via email or a prominent notice on our website. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
