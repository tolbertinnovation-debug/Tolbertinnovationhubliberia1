We believe you deserve to know exactly what to expect at every stage. Whether you are applying for a scholarship, seeking healthcare abroad, launching a software project, or joining our free classroom: here is how it works.

Scholarship Process

How we guide you from first inquiry to admission letter and beyond

1

Eligibility Check

We assess your academic background, language proficiency, and target country to identify the scholarship programs that are the right fit for your goals and qualifications.

2

Document Preparation

Our team guides you through gathering and verifying every required document: transcripts, recommendation letters, personal statements, medical certificates, and more, ensuring everything meets official standards.

3

Application Submission

We prepare and review your complete application package before submission, checking every detail and deadline to maximize your chances of a successful outcome.

4

Admission Letter

Once accepted, we assist with your official admission letter, student visa application, pre-departure orientation, and all the practical steps needed before you fly out to begin your studies.

🏥

Healthcare Process

How we coordinate your medical journey from case inquiry to successful treatment

1

Case Inquiry

You share your medical situation with us: diagnosis, reports, and treatment history. Our healthcare coordinator reviews your case and identifies which specialist hospitals and countries are best suited for your condition.

2

Medical Assessment

We forward your medical records to our partner hospitals for a preliminary remote assessment and cost estimate. Our team explains the findings and recommendations in plain language so you can make an informed decision.

3

Specialist Referral

We issue a formal referral letter to the chosen hospital and coordinate appointment scheduling, medical visa documentation, and all the administrative requirements needed for your travel clearance.

4

Travel & Treatment

Our team assists with travel bookings, airport pickup arrangements at the destination, and stays in regular contact throughout your treatment. We continue to follow up after your return to ensure a full recovery.

💻

Software & IT Process

How we turn your idea into a fully delivered digital product

1

Consultation Request

Submit a brief description of your project: what you need, who will use it, and any deadlines. We acknowledge every request within 24 hours and schedule a discovery session at your convenience.

2

Discovery Call

In a structured 30–60 minute session, our software team digs into your requirements, existing systems, user base, and technical constraints to fully understand the scope before recommending a solution.

3

Proposal

We deliver a clear written proposal covering the recommended solution, technology stack, project timeline, milestones, pricing, and support terms. No surprises, no vague estimates.

4

Development & Delivery

Our team builds your solution in agreed sprints with regular progress updates. Final delivery includes testing, handover documentation, staff training, and an agreed post-launch support period.

Free Learning Hub Process

How to start learning IELTS, TOEFL, and more, completely free

1

Choose Your Pathway

Select either the IELTS or TOEFL preparation pathway, or explore both. Each pathway is structured into clear modules covering every exam component from orientation through advanced test strategies.

2

Follow the Modules

Work through video lessons, reading materials, writing and speaking exercises, and listening practice at your own pace. Practice tests are available throughout so you can track your progress in real time.

3

Complete & Certify

Upon completing all required modules and assessments, request your Tolbert Innovation Hub Certificate of Completion, a recognized credential you can present alongside your scholarship or university application.
