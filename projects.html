International Healthcare Referral Network

We operate a growing network of specialist hospitals in India and partner countries, providing Liberians with access to cardiac, oncology, orthopaedic, and other specialist care not available locally. Every patient referral includes full coordination from medical visa to follow-up.

Free IELTS / TOEFL Digital Classroom

Our fully digital, self-paced classroom delivers structured IELTS and TOEFL preparation through six module pathways per course. With video lessons, practice tests, study guides, and completion certificates, it is one of the most comprehensive free English exam resources in Liberia.
