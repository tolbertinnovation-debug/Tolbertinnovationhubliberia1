Scholarship Program

Eligibility & Application Requirements

Our scholarship pathway supports students from eligibility assessment through admission letter issuance.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Liberian student or recent graduate with valid identification
  • Academic transcripts/certificates from recognized institutions
  • Personal statement explaining study goals and impact plan
  • Valid contact information for interview and updates

Application Steps

  1. Submit online scholarship form and required documents.
  2. Program team reviews eligibility and conducts follow-up screening.
  3. Shortlisted applicants receive guidance for partner school matching.
  4. Qualified candidates proceed to admission letter processing.

Process

Admission Letter Issuance Process

01. Verification

All submitted academic and personal records are reviewed for completeness and eligibility.

02. Institutional Matching

Applicants are matched with suitable partner institutions and programs.

03. Partner Submission

Documents are prepared and forwarded to relevant institutions for decision processing.

04. Admission Letter Delivery

Successful candidates receive official admission letters and next-step guidance.
