Financial Support

Scholarships & Funding Opportunities

Multiple funding options are available for qualified Liberian students — from full tuition waivers to merit awards.

🏆

Merit Scholarship

Up to 50% off

Awarded to students with strong academic records (GPA 3.0+ or distinction). Available at most partner universities in India.

🌍

ICCR Fully Funded

100% Funded

Government of India scholarship covering tuition, stipend, airfare, visa, and accommodation for the India-Africa Maitri initiative.

🏅

Sports Scholarship

Up to 30% off

For students with outstanding achievements in football, athletics, basketball, or other recognized competitive sports.

💡

Early Admission Award

Up to 25% off

Students who confirm enrollment before the early deadline receive reduced tuition rates at select partner universities.

🎓

Women in STEM Grant

Up to 40% off

Dedicated funding for female students pursuing Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics programs.

🤝

Sibling Discount

10–15% off

Where two or more siblings apply to the same partner university, both receive a loyalty tuition reduction on annual fees.
