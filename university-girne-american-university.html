About Girne American University

Girne American University (GAU) is one of Cyprus's largest and most internationally recognized universities, founded in 1988 in Nicosia, the island's capital. With 10,000+ students from over 100 countries, GAU offers 200+ programs across medicine, engineering, law, business, architecture, and more. GAU is one of the very few universities in Cyprus that offers a full Faculty of Medicine and a Faculty of Pharmacy, making it a top destination for Liberian students interested in health sciences. All programs are delivered in English.

Campus Life

GAU's modern campus sits in the heart of Nicosia, offering students state-of-the-art facilities including a teaching hospital, dental clinic, advanced laboratories, a central library, and extensive sports facilities. The city blends Mediterranean charm with a cosmopolitan lifestyle, making student life both academically enriching and culturally vibrant.
