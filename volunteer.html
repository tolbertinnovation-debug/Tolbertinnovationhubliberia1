Join Our Mission to Transform Lives in Africa

At Tolbert Innovation Hub, we believe transformation happens through people: passionate, committed people who share their knowledge, time, and skills with those who need it most. Whether you are an educator, a technologist, a healthcare professional, or a storyteller, there is a role here for you.

Why It Matters

Why Volunteer with Tolbert Innovation Hub?

Volunteering with us is not just about giving back. It is about being part of a community building something lasting and real in Liberia and across Africa.

🌟

Create Real, Lasting Impact

Your hours directly benefit students pursuing scholarships, families navigating healthcare, and entrepreneurs growing their businesses. You will see the difference you make in concrete, human terms.

🌍

Join a Global Network

Tolbert Innovation Hub connects volunteers with a community that spans Liberia and the diaspora: educators, technologists, healthcare professionals, and advocates all working toward the same vision.

📈

Grow Your Own Skills

Volunteering challenges you to teach, communicate, mentor, and lead. Many of our volunteers report that their time with us has sharpened their own professional and personal skills in unexpected ways.

Open Positions

Volunteer Roles Available

We have structured roles across all our program areas. Roles can be remote or in-person, ongoing or project-based. All volunteers receive orientation and ongoing support from our team.

📖
Education Mentor
2–4 hrs/week · Remote-friendly

Support students preparing for IELTS or TOEFL examinations. You will guide learners through difficult module concepts, provide feedback on practice writing and speaking tasks, and help them build the confidence they need to perform on exam day.

IELTS/TOEFL knowledge Teaching Patient communication
🎓
Scholarship Advisor
3–5 hrs/week · Hybrid

Guide scholarship applicants through the application process, from eligibility review and document checklist management through essay guidance, deadline tracking, and final submission support. Ideal for anyone with knowledge of international scholarship programs or admissions processes.

Scholarship knowledge Counseling Attention to detail
💻
Technology Trainer
3–6 hrs/week · Remote or In-person

Deliver digital skills training to small business owners and entrepreneurs in Liberia. Topics include basic website management, social media for business, digital marketing, and foundational productivity tools. No advanced technical background needed, just practical knowledge and a willingness to teach.

Digital skills Training delivery Business understanding
🏥
Healthcare Coordinator
2–4 hrs/week · Remote or Local

Assist with patient referral coordination by helping families understand their options, organize required medical documentation, and navigate the process of accessing care abroad. Healthcare or medical administration background preferred, but compassion and organizational skills are equally important.

Healthcare knowledge Documentation Empathy
✏️
Content Creator
Flexible · Fully Remote

Develop educational materials, study guides, blog posts, and social media content that supports our programs and extends our reach. If you can write clearly, explain complex concepts simply, and create content that resonates with a Liberian and African student audience, we want to hear from you.

Writing Content strategy Educational design
📣
Community Outreach
Flexible · Monrovia-based preferred

Help spread awareness of Tolbert Innovation Hub's programs to schools, community centers, youth groups, and neighborhoods across Liberia. This role is about being a bridge between students and families who need our services and the programs that can help them. Strong communication skills and community connections are your key tools.

Community networks Communication Outreach experience

The Process

How to Become a Volunteer

Joining as a volunteer is straightforward. We review every application carefully and respond within five business days.

1
Submit Your Application

Fill out the volunteer application form below with your background, interests, and availability. Tell us which role excites you most and what you hope to contribute.

2
Brief Orientation Call

Our team will reach out to schedule a short call, usually 20–30 minutes, to discuss your application, answer your questions, and confirm the best role fit for your skills and schedule.

3
Start Making Impact

Once confirmed, you will receive access to our volunteer resources, be connected with your program team, and begin contributing within the week. Welcome to the family.
