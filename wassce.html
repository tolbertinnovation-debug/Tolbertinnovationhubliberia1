📖 Proven WAEC Strategies

6 Tips to Score Higher on WASSCE

Based on WAEC chief examiner reports and TIH tutor experience helping hundreds of Liberian students pass.

📋

Use Past Questions

WAEC repeats topics every 3–4 years. Solve past questions from 2018–2024 to spot patterns and boost confidence.

📘

Know the Syllabus

Download the official WAEC syllabus. Topics outside it are never tested. Focus your time on what counts.

⏱️

Timed Practice

Simulate real exam conditions. 30 minutes per essay in a 2.5-hour paper. Practice this weekly.

👥

Study Groups (Max 4)

Teaching a topic to a peer is the fastest way to master it. Keep groups small and focused.

😴

Sleep Well

Memory consolidates during sleep. Never pull an all-nighter before your exam. Aim for 7–8 hours.

✏️

Show All Working

In Paper 2 subjects like Maths and Physics, partial marks are awarded for correct method. Never skip steps.
